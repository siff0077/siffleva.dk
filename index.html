Who am I?

...a curious multimediadesign student

My name is Siff Leva Jensen, I'm 25 years old. I study multimediadesign at KEA, I'm currently on my 3rd semester, which is all about front-end design.

My goal is to create engaging user-experiences and exicitng web-solutions. I find joy in working with the smallest details and play around with the visual expression to create facinating graphic designs.

I'm constantly amazed with how much you can create, when combining creativity and "nerdy" code. I'm eager to learn more about front-end design and gain a lot of experience!

As a person, I'm very outgoing, like to talk a lot, but most importantly I'm curious! In my sparetime, you'll find me either by my computer working on illustration or just playing around with webdesign and code or running around with my camera playing amateur-photographer, which is also a big passion of mine!

So if you have a project that you think I could be helpful with, then feel free to contact me !
