Who am I?

...a curious multimediadesign student

My name is Siff Leva Jensen, I'm 25 years old. I study multimediadesign at KEA, I'm currently on my 3rd semester, which is all about front-end design.

As a person, I'm very outgoing, like to talk a lot, but most importantly I'm curious and eager to learn more about front-end design and gain a lot of experince!

I feel like I've finally found "the right shelf" for myself. I'm constantly amazed with how much you can create, when combining creativity, UI- & UX-design and "nerdy" structered code.

In my sparetime, you'll find me either by my computer working on illustration or just playing around with webdesign and code or running around with my camera playing amateur-photographer, which is also a big passion of mine!

So if you have a project that you think I could be helpful with, then feel free to contact me !
