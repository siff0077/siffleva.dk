Websites

Websites can be fun, creative, fresh, cool, modern, clean, simple and much more. A good website captures the users attention, have a clear communication, gives the user a good experince and makes them come back.

My goal is to create expressive websites with great UI and UX, which hopefully will give the users the best experince possible.

V-Studio | School project

V-studio is a freelance business founded by Andreea Vlad. She is an artist and a graphic designer.

For our 2nd semester exam my studygroup and I took on the callenge of creating a website for her newly started company. V-Studio didn't have any online presence - Andreea wanted the website to portray her as a graphic designer and communicate her skills to potentiel clients.

On of the critirias for the project was to create a video either for the website or as a SoMe-element. We chose to create a video, which was to be placed on V-Studios future LinkedIn profile, but we also created a little splash-video for the website to personalize the site and engaged potentiel costumers.

IHS | School project

This project was made on my 2nd semester as a group-project. We were to create a new and improved information structure for IHS | Idrætshøjskolen Sønderborg.

They have a lot of information on their current website, so we had to create a better experience for the user by changed the navigation, the general look and number of sub-pages.

We also tried to make the expression of the website appealing to the specific user-group, which meant that we had to make a lot of usertests.

Portfolio | 1st semester | School project

This was my first portfolio, which I made for my 1st semester exam. I went for a fresh and playful look, which I think I manage to create. I made several user-tests to make sure I got the expression right.

The portfolio contains all my projects from my 1st semester on Multimediadesign. The site is made mainly with HTML and CSS, it only uses JavaScript to control the burgermenu with 'toggle'.

The portfolio has some flaws, which should be corrected, if I were to use it. Eg. the text on the frontpage should not be as wide as the full view-width, the responsiveness of the website could also be corrected a bit, especially for bigger viewports.

Redesign project | School project

This was a redesign project, which was a group assignment on my 1st semester.

We had to find a website, which we thought could use an update. We chose Frederiksberg Volley, because one of our groupmembers played volleyball it that club, adn we thought that we could create a nice redesign of their website.

We made a lot of research and user-test to create a new website for the club, but at the same time we had to maintain the feeling the club wanted their users to experience when visiting the site.

Sugar Rush | School project

This website was made as an assignment on my 2nd semester. It was a group assignment, and it was one of my first encounters with grid, and how to fetch JSON-data in JavaScript.

We had to find something to make a site about, my group and I chose desserts. Who doesn't like desserts?

We fetched the JSON-data with an async-function and inserted it a grid-view in our HTML. We also created a pop-up, so that you could see the recipes for each dessert.
